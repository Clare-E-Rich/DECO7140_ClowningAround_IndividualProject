Me & That

This class teaches a range of basic circus skills that require equipment. Participants will be able to experience stilts, trampolining, unicycling and hula-hooping. They can then choose to focus on one activity for the rest of the term or to work across all of the skills. Participants not only build the strength, co-ordination and dedication necessary to advance to higher level circus performance, but also work with their peers towards a group performance at the end of the term.
