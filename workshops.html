Workshops

Looking for a workshop for your team?

Circus is great for fitness and creativity, teambuilding and lateral thinking.

As a non-competitive, physical activity requiring trust and communication, circus is a wonderful way for any team or group to come together.

Our program can be administered in our space or somewhere of your choosing and can be tailored to meet a variety of different outcomes based on what you and your organization require.

We can offer anything from a one hour juggling workshop to help break up a conference or training day, all the way to a full 6 hour day filled with trapeze, pole, silks, acrobatics, unicycling and many other circus disciplines.

Call or email so that we can work out the best session for you!
