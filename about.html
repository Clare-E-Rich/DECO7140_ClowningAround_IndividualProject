About

"Spontaneous, fearless and playful, let your clown burst forth!"

Clowning Around is a school which offers classes in the ancient and nobel art of clowning. We believe that there is a clown inside everyone and we want to work with you to bring out your inner clown.

Our trainers are lifelong members of the circus community with over 100 years experience between them. We are experts at coaxing the inner clown out of everyone and using this joyful engery to build strength, dexterity and confidence.

Our classes cater to all ages and all skill levels, from beginner to expert.
